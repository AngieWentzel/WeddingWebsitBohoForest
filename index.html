Our Story

How We Met

Our story began five years ago at a friend's autumn bonfire. Elena was trying to light a fire that just wouldn't catch, and James, a self-proclaimed outdoorsman, offered to help. We spent the whole evening talking by the fire, and despite James' questionable fire-making skills (it went out three times), we found a spark that never faded.

The Proposal

On a misty morning hike to our favorite forest overlook, James surprised Elena with a picnic breakfast. As the sun broke through the trees, he got down on one knee amidst the wildflowers and asked the most important question of his life. Of course, she said yes—after the initial shock wore off!
